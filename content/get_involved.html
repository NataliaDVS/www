Get Involved
Love the sound of Open Food?
Keep in touch - For more information about the project - including if / when you can buy food on the Open Food Network in your area - sign up for our mailing list.
Developing and trialling the software - The Open Food Network code is released under an open source license (AGPL) and is available here. The first instance of the hosted Open Food Network is currently being trialled and developed with limited users in Australia.
Become a member or make a donation - Contribute to the open food revolution by becoming a member of the Open Food Foundation. Your contribution will help us build awesome stuff, faster - and will give you a say in what gets built next. Annual membership starts at $50 a year. Donations of any size welcome! Sign up to find out how.
Volunteer - We welcome volunteers of all shapes and sizes - technical (especially rails developers) and non-tech people alike. While we are physically based in Melbourne, there are plenty of opportunities to contribute from wherever you are in the world. Sign up to tell us your interests.
