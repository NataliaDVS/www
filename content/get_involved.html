Get Involved
Love the sound of Open Food?
When you sign up to be on our mailing list we will:
  1. Provide you with regular updates including when you can buy food through the Open Food Network in your area.
  2. Give you opportunities to get involved in other ways (by joining us as a trial partner, or contributing time, skills or money).
  3. Keep your information confidential.
We welcome any help from developers out there - the Open Food Network code is released under an open source license (AGPL) and is available here.
